About Us | God’s Cares Nurturing Home

At God’s Cares Nurturing Home, we believe every child is a precious gift and deserves a safe, loving, and stimulating environment to grow. Founded with a heart for children and a passion for early childhood development, our daycare is more than just a place to stay — it’s a second home where little minds are nurtured, little hands are guided, and little hearts feel loved.

Our Story

God’s Cares Nurturing Home started from a simple vision: to provide parents with peace of mind while giving children the care, attention, and learning foundation they need to thrive. What began as a small family initiative has grown into a trusted space where children are treated like family, and parents are treated like partners.

Why Parents Trust Us

  • Safe & Secure Environment: Fully child-proofed, clean, and monitored 24/7
  • Qualified & Caring Staff: Trained caregivers with genuine love and patience
  • Holistic Learning Approach: Play, creativity, and structured learning
  • Faith-Based Values: Kindness, respect, honesty, and gratitude
  • Healthy Meals & Routine: Balanced meals and structured daily routine

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What age group do you accept?

We accept children from 3 months to 7 years old.

2. What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Friday, 7:00am – 6:30pm. Extended care until 7:30pm available. Open on Saturday and public holidays.

3. How do you ensure the safety of the children?

Fully child-proofed facility, secure entry, 24/7 monitoring, and trained staff in first aid and CPR. Only authorized persons can pick up a child.

4. What if my child is sick?

We cannot accept a child with fever, contagious illness, or vomiting. Parents will be contacted immediately if a child falls sick.

5. How do you handle discipline?

Positive discipline focused on kindness, redirection, and teaching good behavior. No physical punishment.

6. Can I visit or check on my child during the day?

Absolutely. Parents are welcome. We share daily updates and photos via WhatsApp.

7. What should my child bring to daycare?

Change of clothes, diapers, labeled water bottle, food, snacks, and comfort item. Full checklist given at registration.

8. How do I register my child?

Visit us or contact via phone/WhatsApp to schedule a tour. We’ll provide registration form and discuss fees.

9. What are your fees and payment terms?

Fees depend on age and care type. Payment monthly in advance. 5% discount for siblings.

10. Do you teach Christian values?

Yes. We gently teach kindness, respect, sharing, and gratitude through stories, songs, and activities.
